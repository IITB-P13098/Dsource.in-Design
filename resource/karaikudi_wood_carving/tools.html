Wood Carving in Karaikudi

Crafts of design with majestic look and rigidity
by
Professor Bibhudutta Baral
NID R&D Campus, Bangalore

Tools and Raw Materials:

Different types of tools and raw materials are used for engraving. The type of wood usually preferred for engraving work are- teak, rose and vengai wood (locally called). The chisels are called as "Ulli" in (local language). The woodcarvings made in this place have flair in the styles. Polishing is done to give the product more attractive look and to seal the surface against moisture.

Wooden Mallet:
Beating is done using to give shape to wood.

Chisels:
Different types of chisels varying from size and shapes are used for carving.

Scale and Pencil:
It is used o mark and draw designs.

KG Cardboard:
To make designs.

Carbon sheets:
It is used to trace the designs as reference.

L - shaped Ruler:
It is used to measure the edges of the wood.

Router Machine:
It is used to scrape out the unwanted areas in the design.

Wax:
Used to polish the article giving it more elegant appearance.

Sand paper:
It is used to make the surface smooth.
