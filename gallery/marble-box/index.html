Marble Box

Documented by Astha Kabra, Industrial Design Centre (IDC), IIT Bombay, kabraastha@gmail.com

Marble crafting from agra is intricately designed and delicately carved with aesthetically beautiful and functional utility. Marble embellishment is found mostly with stones, acrylic paint, kundan and gold paintings. This inlay art is striking and attractive. These beautiful boxes can be used for multi purpose reasons.
